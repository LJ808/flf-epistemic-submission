# Díez-Espino et al. 2017 — PREDIMED substudy

High-CV-risk Mediterranean cohort, ~50% diabetic. Non-diabetic HR for >4 eggs/week: 0.96 (CI 0.33–2.76). Diabetic HR: 1.33 (CI 0.72–2.46). Formal interaction test by diabetic status: not significant.
