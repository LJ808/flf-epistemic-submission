# Zhong/Drouin-Chartier et al. 2020 — BMJ, three-cohort pooled analysis

NHS, NHS-II, HPFS cohorts. n=215,618. Up to 32 years follow-up, 14,806 incident CVD cases. Multivariable-adjusted: ≥1 egg/day not significantly associated with incident CVD after adjustment for updated lifestyle/dietary factors.
