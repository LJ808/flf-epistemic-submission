# Li et al. 2013 — Atherosclerosis meta-analysis

14 studies, n=320,778. Pooled RR for CVD (highest vs lowest egg intake): 1.19. Pooled RR for CVD in diabetics specifically: 1.83. Pooled RR for diabetes: 1.68.
